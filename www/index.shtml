This is the first comprehensive textbook of this fascinating interdisciplinary topic which explains advances in a way that it is easily accessible to engineering, physics and math students.

It presents practical applications of traffic theory such as driving behavior, stability analysis, stop-and-go waves, and travel time estimation. These topics are presented in a novel and systematic way by addressing both microscopic and macroscopic models with a focus on traffic instabilities.

This textbook is a revised and extended edition of the German textbook Verkehrsdynamik und -simulation. Find out more about the book.
